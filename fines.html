Fines

Description

Displays a list of all fines associated with this account. Information includes:

  • Title - The item for/to which the fine was assigned.
  • Date - The date that this fine was levied.
  • Amount - The amount of the fine (USD).
  • Type - The reason for the fine. Reasons can include:
    • Overdue - The item was not returned or renewed by the due date.
    • Lost Item Processing - The administrative costs of replacing a lost item???????????
    • Forgive - The listed fine was cancelled.
    • Lost Item Replacement - The item was reported as lost, and must be replaced.

Usage

  • To view fines:
    • Fines will appear on the screen, sorted by date. Scroll up or down to view the entire list of fines.
    • A "Total" is displayed at the top of the screen. This represents the sum total of all fines for this account.
  • To return to the previous menu:
    • Click the “Home” icon or use the back arrow.
